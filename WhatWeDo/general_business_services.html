General Business Services

The attorneys at Mag Mile Law know that owning a business can be tough. It can be demanding. We know that the ups and downs of business can be difficult. We know that business owners have sleepless nights worrying about the business, about making money, about their employees, about the competition or about anything else that might affect the bottom line.

If you're a business owner with the attorneys at Mag Mile Law on your side, you won't have to worry about the law. The attorneys at Mag Mile Law have a wealth of experience representing businesses large and small with routine and complex issues. We take pride in understanding each client's business - top to bottom, inside and out. Our clients range from Fortune 100 CEOs to closely held family businesses. We understand that no matter what type of business you have, you will want your lawyers to know how important that business is to you, and who are willing to work tirelessly on your behalf.

Our attorneys have the background, experience, and skills necessary to address your business needs. We routinely counsel clients on entity formation (LLC's, corporations, partnerships), business sales and purchases, business dissolution, business mergers and business litigation.

Many business attorneys never step foot in the courtroom. Not us. Our attorneys are skilled negotiators and experienced trial attorneys. As trial attorneys, we can quickly identify potential issues with a business transaction and provide counsel on how to address those issues using our wealth of courtroom experience. With the attorneys at Mag Mile Lawn on your side, you will know that our advice and counsel is based on our years of courtroom experience, and we will not hesitate to go to court on your behalf if necessary.

Let us work together to help you grow your business. Contact us for a free consultation today.
