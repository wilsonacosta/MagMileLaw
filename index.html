WHAT SEPERATES US FROM THE REST?

When looking for someone to represent you, look no further than the attorneys at Mag Mile Law. With over 60 years of combined experience representing clients in various types of matters, the attorneys at Mag Mile Law have built a reputation as trusted counselors, dedicated advocates and experienced attorneys. The following cases reflect a sample of the results we have obtained for our clients throughout the years. While these results should not be taken as promises for your case, they reflect the skills we have both inside and outside the courtroom.

RESULTS

  • Rolls v. Malitz: 02 L 14385 – Jury Verdict in favor of client (plaintiff personal injury)
  • Hercules Iron Works v. 1330 S. Michigan Avenue Development: 2011 CH 04576 – Jury Verdict in favor of client (breach of contract tried in Law Division)
  • Christopoulos v. TBS African Restaurant: 2012 M1 728021 – Jury Verdict in favor of client
  • Tsai v. Housakos: 2013 L 3141 – Bench trial verdict in favor of client (fraud, unjust enrichment claims)
  • Evlogia GE. LLC v. I Love Chicago, Inc. 2017 CH 01984 Bench trial verdict in favor of client (declaratory judgment, fraud claims)
  • Inter Rail Systems Inc. v. Ravi Corporation – 387 Ill App. 3d 510 - Motion for summary judgment affirmed against client (mechanics lien)
  • Santorini Cab Corporation v. Cross Town Cab Company - 2012 IL App (1st) 110428-U – Trial court opinion affirmed. (specific performance and attorney fee provision analysis)
  • Tsai v. Picoulas – 2018 IL App (1 st ) 162531-U – Bench trial in favor of client affirmed.
  • Crawford v. Belhaven Realty, LLC – 2018 IL App (1 st ) 170731 – Summary judgment entered against client reversed (assumption of liability, indemnification, mitigation issues)
